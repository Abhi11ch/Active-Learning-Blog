University Adoption

Many institutions are transforming their learning environments and curriculum to integrate active learning methodologies. This includes physical classroom redesign and digital innovations.

  • Redesigning classrooms with flexible seating and technology
  • Training faculty to facilitate student-centered teaching
  • Integrating active learning tools in online platforms
  • Assessing outcomes to continuously improve teaching methods
