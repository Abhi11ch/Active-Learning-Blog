Popular Techniques

Several well-established strategies help educators implement active learning in the classroom effectively. These techniques foster student interaction and deepen understanding.

  • Think-Pair-Share: Students think individually, then discuss with a partner, and share with the class.
  • Peer Teaching: Students explain concepts to each other.
  • Problem-Based Learning (PBL): Students learn by solving real-world problems.
  • Interactive Simulations: Students engage with dynamic, hands-on learning environments.
