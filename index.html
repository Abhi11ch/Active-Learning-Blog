What is Active Learning?

Active learning is a method of instruction that actively engages students in the learning process, making them participants rather than passive listeners. This approach can include reading, writing, discussion, or problem-solving that promotes analysis, synthesis, and evaluation of class content.

  • Encourages student participation
  • Focuses on student-centered teaching
  • Improves conceptual understanding
